# Privacy Policy for FAP-GPA-Viewer

_Last updated: October 2025_

## Overview

FAP-GPA-Viewer is an **unofficial, open-source Chrome Extension** built for educational and personal use by students of FPT University.  
The project operates entirely **client-side** and does **not** collect, transmit, or store personal data on any external server.

---

## 1. Data Collection

This extension:
- **Does not collect** usernames, passwords, student IDs, or any other personal identifiers.  
- **Does not transmit** any data outside your device.  
- **Does not use cookies** or third-party analytics.
